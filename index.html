About

Moment is a wearable device that can be worn on the face as a device that simultaneously provides both the original function of glasses to see objects in front and the function of a computer. Moment can solve the inconvenience that consumers have to use smart devices with their hands by using the strength of hands-free.

Moment:

  • It is designed with built-in camera to solve the inconvenience of consumers' needs. It is for those of who want to enjoy the scene with their eyes and keep them as photos or videos at the same time. In addition, pupil recognition function captures the moment of user's perspective as a photo or video.
  • It can be commanded by voice and gesture recognition, and the application has photo and video editing capabilities. Therefor, users don't need to download any other photo/video edittor additionally.
  • It with AR function can re-experience the scenes that users have experienced using the photos or videos they have taken. Or they can enjoy their own design with AR function. In addition, it is possible to change the color of the glasses through the application.
  • It supports wireless communication technologies such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, and can search and share information in real time through an Internet connection. In addition, user can check the time, phone calls, text messages, maps, and navigation that they need to check on their smartphone with voice commands through your glasses.

Features:

COMFORT Provide a sense of weight and a natural fit that is not conscious of wearing, such as clothes and accessories used in everyday life.
REACT ABILITY Application support that can support continuous communication between a computer and a user to provide an immediate response to user requests.
INTERFACE Provides users with a natural sense of unity and integration to expand human physical and intellectual abilities.
STABILITY Minimize discomfort and physical fatigue caused by long-term wear, and ensure stability against power and electromagnetic waves.
SOCIALITY Possible to protect the privacy of individuals and forms that conform to sociocultural conventional wisdom, excluding cultural disparity caused by wearing.
